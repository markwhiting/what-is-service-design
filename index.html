What is Service Design?

First, the basics:

Imagine two different auto repair shops — both can quickly and cheaply fix your car. At the end of the day, the one with a better-designed service will provide you with the best experience, and you’ll be more likely to recommend it to friends and family.

  1. Services are intangible goods; and just like cars and websites, they can be well designed.
  2. Because of this intangibility, people are less aware of services, and often overlook the quality of their design.

Why was this made?

Service Design is a young field that can be tricky to explain, but we believe that it can be done well.

Whether you’re a designer in another field, a potential business client, or just an interested individual, this video was created to help you understand the process, role, and values of Service Design.
